How to Order

1. Order online

Click on the ORDER HERE button above to start your online order. We recommend ordering early for the best selection. If you need to cancel your order, please call the shop at 401-861-5190.

Learn more about Thanksgiving ordering

2. Pickup your order

Pick up your order in-store. Keep in mind that preorders pick up and take out are at the same line. If you prefer a shorter waiting time, we recommend coming in the morning. There is no need to call us if you are running a little late!

Learn more about Thanksgiving hours

3. Enjoy your pastries!

We hope you enjoy our homemade pastries. When bringing cakes home, make sure to keep them in a cooler or insulated bag if you are traveling for longer periods of time. We sell insulated bags that keep products cold for 1-2 hours. Let us know if you need one!
